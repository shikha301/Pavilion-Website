Welcome to our Pavilion!

Starting from this January we are calling to our Pavilion, 10 startups and the nimble teams behind them for an intensive 4 month head together aimed at energising their initiatives beyond the walls of mind, ideas and execution.

In case you or someone you know has been passionate about a startup or an idea, this may just be the place they need to enter to realise their dreams.

About Us

What is most notable is the emerging clan of start-ups with the assistance of innovation and technology which together are the real drivers for the growth of the Indian economy.

Many start-ups are current Unicorns belonging from India and soon there will be hundreds more. This neo-entrepreneur culture is the biggest hope for the most challenging phenomena of unemployment and also the source of wealth creation.

It’s time to further boost this ecosystem to ensure that this century belongs to India.

Whom are we looking for?

  • Someone with dedication, perseverance and a mighty heart with a can-do and never-give-up attitude.
  • An early-stage company whose founder is a student and has recently begun his/her/their venture.
  • At least one person in the founding team should be associated with a premier educational institution.
  • In case of a solo/single founder; the person must be ready to increase the size of his founding team to more than one.
  • The Founders should be Resident of India or willing to start their venture here.

What does the founder get?

In addition to 20 lakh in your bank account, you will have Legal, Financial, Managerial, Strategic, Tactical, Technical support, advisory, and mentorship is available to suit the Startup founder’s unique circumstance.

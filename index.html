Welcome to our Venture Brainery!

Starting from this January we are calling to our Pavilion, 5 startups and the nimble teams behind them for an intensive 4 month head together aimed at energising their initiatives beyond the walls of mind, ideas and execution.

In case you or someone you know has been passionate about a startup or an idea, this may just be the place they need to enter to realise their dreams.

What does the founder get?

In addition to 20 lakh in your bank account, you will have Legal, Financial, Managerial, Strategic, Tactical, Technical support, advisory, and mentorship is available to suit the Startup founder’s unique circumstance.

The mentors are people who have been in the startup ecosystem for over a decade across different geographies and know all there is to know about the challenges that a startup faces and how to overcome them.

This is a program with no other parallel where we shall handhold you when you need it and let you do your own thing if that is working for you.

You will have a community of people backing you. Most of whom have “been there and done that”; from someone who was the President of a stock exchange body at 23 to another who is building a unicorn venture in robotics and AI.

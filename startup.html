Our Mission

We are building an eco-system of budding entrepreneurs and start-up enthusiasts under a GURUKUL framework.

“Aiding your hard work and ingenuity with our comprehensive support from capital to knowledge and networking opportunities, we aim to realize your idea to reality!”

Our Service

Provide & Start-up

Provide mentorship and help build a core team for the start-up.

Provide & Support

Provide shape to the organization and administration support from structuring, hiring, legal advisory and any other service that an early age start-up requires.

Provide & Funding

Provide with scaling, fund raising and merger opportunities.

Our Investment Criterion

  • Young, budding founders possessing < 2 years of work experience.
  • Aspiring founders with quality educational background from top notch institutes.
  • Morally correct founders who follow Code of Ethics.
  • Start-ups with sinless and ethical business, having raised < 200k INR.
  • Flexible founder, willing to expand core team.

Our Goals

01.

Investing up to 30 Lakh INR in well-researched and capable portfolio of start-ups in next 6 months.

02.

Build a strong, dedicated and skilled team of analysts, community & partnership managers.

03.

Focus on partnership development and organize events and programs for the benefit of start-up.

Deal Evaluation Methodology

01.

Proprietary Framework for assessment and identification of future prospects and risk-taking structure.

02.

Idea evaluation and Entry and Exit Valuation and Opportunities.

03.

Promissory notes signing for supporting other Pavilion companies.

04.

Idea development and preparation for international accelerators (e.g., YC, TechStars)

Monitoring and Risk Management

1.

Warrants/Pro Rata & ROFR Rights, Partnerships & Co Investing.

2.

Weekly interaction to discuss and resolve common issues and benchmarking during office hours.

3.

Regular monitoring of competition and traction analysis by hiring interns.

4.

Co-Development and Support partnerships with other portfolio companies to minimize and mitigate risks (around 100 hours).

5.

Periodic monitoring and engagement with operating team and relationship managers in the working hours.

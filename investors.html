Our Mission

We are building an eco-system of budding entrepreneurs and startup enthusiasts under a GURUKUL framework.

“With decades of experience in running successful businesses and selecting start-ups, we reduce possibilities of failure and provide you with a safer investment haven.”

What do we do?

  • Facilitate investments into strong start-ups with exponential growth opportunities after hand-picking them by means of a time tested framework.
  • Assemble, align and establish a strong team by helping them blossom with access to mentorship and ecosystem routinely beyond their reach.
  • We will help you to build, operate and manage your portfolios.
  • Thorough management and monitoring of investment life-cycle.
  • Conduct negotiations for sales of securities.

Our Investment Criterion

Experience

Young, budding founders possessing < 2 years of work experience.

Education

Aspiring founders with quality educational background from top notch institutes and deep market insite

Support

Morally conscious founders who follow a Code of Honour and Ethics.

Raising

Start-ups with sinless and ethical business, having raised right for early stage investment.

Founder

Flexible founder, willing to expand core team.

Our Goals

  • Build a strong, dedicated and skilled team of analysts, community & partnership managers.
  • Investing in well-researched and capable portfolio of start-ups in next 6 months.
  • Focus on partnership development and organize a demo day for all the campus funds and small angel groups.
  • Performance review in 6 months and setting up a structured alternative investment fund (AIF), alongside an incubator.
  • To support and work with 50 start ups by the end of 2022.
